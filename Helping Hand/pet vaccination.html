Importance of Vaccination

Vaccinating your dog is crucial for preventing various infectious diseases. It not only protects your pet but also helps in controlling the spread of diseases to other animals and humans. Regular vaccinations ensure a healthy and happy life for your furry friend.

Vaccination Schedule

Age Vaccine Notes
6-8 weeks Distemper, Parvovirus First dose
10-12 weeks Distemper, Parvovirus, Hepatitis Second dose
14-16 weeks Rabies First dose
Every 1-2 years Rabies Booster dose
