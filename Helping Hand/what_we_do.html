What we do

Helping Hand is more than just spay/neuter and rescue.

It is about changing a community. Learn more about our holistic approach to end the human/street dog conflict and stop the suffering.

Animal Birth Control or CNVR
End Rabies by 2030
Street Animal Rescue
Humane Education
DAR has implemented a humane and effective program for creating a healthy sustained population of stray dogs which directly impacts community health
Each year we run a 10-day mass vaccination and awareness camp. Throughout the year, every ABC and rescue patient receives their vaccination.
We treat animals in critical condition at our clinic and recovery center. Car accidents, wire traps, illnesses, skin diseases, and maggot wounds are some of the issues we see.
We visit schools and villages to educate them about rabies and compassion towards animals. Our goal is to develop a community partner in caring for street animals.
